Why Visitors Love Our Farm

Four reasons our guests return year after year.

Sustainable Farming

Certified humane practices, free-range grazing, zero chemicals — because we care about the land and our animals.

Hands-On Learning

Every workshop gets you from raw fleece to finished yarn under expert guidance. No experience needed.

Premium Quality

Our alpaca fiber is tested for micron fineness — only the softest, most luxurious fleece reaches our shop.

Family Friendly

From toddlers to grandparents, every age finds joy at the farm. Special kids' tours available every weekend.
