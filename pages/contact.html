Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

❓
What should I wear to the farm?
Comfortable, closed-toe shoes are essential — boots are ideal. Our pastures can be muddy after rain. Dress in layers as Vermont weather changes quickly, especially in spring and fall.
Is the farm accessible for wheelchairs or mobility aids?
We have paved pathways around our main barn area and one accessible pasture viewing area. Please contact us in advance and we'll ensure your visit is comfortable and enjoyable.
Can I bring my dog to the farm?
We love dogs, but for the safety of our alpacas we ask that pets remain in vehicles. Service animals are welcome — please let us know when booking.
Do I need any experience for the fiber arts workshops?
Absolutely not! Our beginner workshops are designed for complete first-timers. Sophie is a patient and encouraging teacher. You'll leave with a finished piece regardless of your starting point.
What's your cancellation policy?
Tours: full refund with 24 hours notice. Workshops: full refund with 48 hours notice. We also offer rain credits for weather-related cancellations on tours, usable within 12 months.
Do you offer private events or corporate team days?
Yes! We love hosting private events — from birthday parties to corporate wellness days. Contact us to discuss a bespoke package for your group of up to 40 people.
