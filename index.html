Our Story

A Family Farm, A Fiber Dream

The Henderson family began raising alpacas in 2012 with a small herd of six on their Vermont meadow. What started as a personal passion for sustainable fiber arts grew into one of New England's most-loved farm experiences.

Today, our 48-strong herd produces some of the finest huacaya and suri fleece in the region — and we can't wait to share it with you.

Certified Humane Practices
Our alpacas live free-range on 40 acres of lush Vermont pasture.
Chemical-Free Fleece
No pesticides, no harsh chemicals — pure, hypoallergenic fiber.
Certified Fiber Artists on Staff
Our workshop instructors are guild-certified master spinners.
